📖 HVAC FLOW FORMULA REFERENCE ACCA · ASHRAE · SMACNA
Airflow
CFM = Area (ft²) × Velocity (FPM)
CFM = Sensible BTU ÷ (1.08 × ΔT)
CFM = Latent BTU ÷ (0.69 × ΔGr)
Area (round) = π × (D/2)² / 144
Area (rect) = (W × H) / 144
Duct Sizing
D (round) = √(CFM × 144 / (π × FPM)) × 2
EqD = 1.3 × (W×H)⁰·⁶²⁵ ÷ (W+H)⁰·²⁵
Friction = 0.109 × V¹·⁸⁵² / D¹·²²⁴ × 100
Sensible Heat
Q sensible = 1.08 × CFM × ΔT
Q latent = 0.69 × CFM × ΔGr
Q total = Sensible + Latent
Tons = BTU/hr ÷ 12,000
SHR = Q sensible ÷ Q total
