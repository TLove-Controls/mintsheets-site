### What You Get

A single `.html` file that opens in any browser. No install. No account. No internet after the first load.

**CFM / Airflow Calculator**
Calculate cubic feet per minute from duct area and velocity, sensible load, latent load, or room square footage. Four methods, one tab.

**Multi-Zone Duct Sizing**
Add as many branches and trunk lines as your system has. Sizes duct diameters using the equal friction method with your target friction rate. Run a full system at once.

**Sensible Heat Load**
CFM and ΔT in. BTU out. Uses the industry-standard 1.08 factor. That's it.

**Static Pressure**
Supply and return readings in, TESP out. Straightforward.

**Live Status Bar**
Real-time feedback as you type — airflow, velocity, and system status update before you lift your finger off the keyboard.
