⚡ Pro Tips — Room CFM
  • Manual J simplified: use 0.75 CFM/ft² for most NW Arkansas homes.
  • ACPH method is ideal for ventilation-critical spaces (kitchens, baths).
  • BTUH method is the most accurate — use post-load calc data when possible.
  • Minimum supply air: 50 CFM per room per ASHRAE 62.2.

⚡ Pro Tips — Duct Sizing
  • Flex duct: derate velocity by ~15% vs. rigid sheet metal for the same CFM.
  • Target face velocity: 500–750 FPM for supply, 400–600 FPM for return.
  • Rule of thumb: 400 CFM per ton → 1-ton system needs ~400 CFM total.
  • Oversized ducts cause low velocity, poor throw, and comfort complaints.
