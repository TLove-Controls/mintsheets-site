⚡ Labor Cost Pro Tips
Know your burdened rate before pricing any job — raw wage is typically only 64–70% of true labor cost. · Set OT billing rates at ≥ 1.3× your standard billable rate to maintain margin on overtime hours. · Use the rate matrix to ensure all time types generate positive margin — drive time at flat rate often loses money. · Review burden percentages quarterly; WC rates can swing significantly based on EMR history.
