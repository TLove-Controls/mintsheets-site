Cookie Policy

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device. They help websites remember preferences and track usage.

Types of Cookies Used

  • Analytics Cookies: for usage metrics and performance.
  • Advertising Cookies: for AdSense ad personalization.
  • Functional Cookies: maintain site behavior and session data.

Google Cookies

We use Google AdSense and DoubleClick cookies. Google collects cookie data to serve relevant ads. See Google's policies for details.

Manage Cookies

  • Use browser settings to delete or block cookies.
  • Opt out of third-party tracking via network tools like Your Online Choices.
  • Data may be less personalized after blocking cookies.
