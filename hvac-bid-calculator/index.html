HVAC Bid Calculator (Estimate, Pricing & Profit Margin Tool)

Create professional estimates, calculate labor burden, and protect your profit margins.

This HVAC bid calculator helps you create professional estimates, calculate labor burden, and protect your profit margins. Combine it with our load calculator for accurate sizing-based bids. Designed for HVAC contractors, technicians, and sales teams.

What This Calculator Does

The HVAC Bid Calculator integrates specialized inputs for equipment, materials, and burdened labor costs to produce a comprehensive project estimate. It utilizes your raw job costs and applies a target gross margin percentage through an interactive slider, allowing you to visually adjust profitability in real-time.

Beyond simple arithmetic, the tool automatically generates a tiered Good, Better, and Best proposal. This functionality helps you present options to customers—like shifting from a standard 14.3 SEER2 unit to a high-efficiency 18 SEER2 system—without having to recalculate three separate bids from scratch.
