Why Break-Even Revenue Matters for HVAC Contractors

The HVAC Break-Even Calculator helps contractors translate monthly overhead and payroll into a clear revenue target. Instead of guessing what the company needs to sell, you can see the sales volume required just to keep the doors open.

Many owners know their overhead and technician payroll, but they do not tie those costs to the actual revenue required per day, per week, or per billable hour. That disconnect leads to underpriced service work, low-margin installations, and revenue goals that sound healthy but still do not cover the full monthly burn rate.

This tool treats overhead and labor as the fixed monthly cost base, then adjusts for material, permit, subcontractor, or other variable costs using a percentage of revenue. If your shop wants to go beyond break-even, the optional target margin field shows the sales goal required to generate profit above those baseline obligations.

Use this together with the Labor Burden Calculator, Service Price Calculator, and Profit Margin Calculator to build a more complete pricing and planning system.
