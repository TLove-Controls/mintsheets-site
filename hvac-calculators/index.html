About MintSheets HVAC Calculators

MintSheets provides the most comprehensive suite of free HVAC calculators available online. Unlike generic engineering tools, every calculator on this site is purpose-built for the HVAC trade — designed around the terminology, workflows, and standards that HVAC technicians, estimators, and contractors actually use in the field.

Our technical calculators align with ACCA Manual J (load calculation), Manual D (duct design), and Manual S (equipment selection) methodology. Our business tools are built around real HVAC contracting economics — labor burden, overhead recovery, flat-rate pricing, and margin protection.

All calculators are completely free to use. No account required, no app download, no paywall. Fully mobile-responsive for use in the field on any smartphone or tablet.

HVAC Calculators for Technicians

Field technicians need fast, accurate tools for commissioning, diagnostics, and service work. The static pressure calculator is essential for diagnosing any airflow complaint or coil icing issue. The flow rate calculator verifies actual blower airflow using temperature rise or static pressure delta. The CFM calculator quickly determines whether a system is delivering design airflow to each room.

HVAC Sizing Tools for Estimators

Proper HVAC sizing requires more than a square footage estimate. The HVAC load calculator uses building envelope data, window area and orientation, and climate zone design temperatures to produce a reliable cooling and heating load estimate. The duct sizing calculator then sizes the air distribution system to match the equipment at an acceptable static pressure budget. The mini split sizing calculator handles ductless zone systems with multi-room BTU allocation.

HVAC Business Calculators for Contractors

Profitability in HVAC contracting requires accurate pricing at every level. The labor burden calculator reveals the true cost of deploying a technician. The bid calculator builds complete job cost estimates with overhead recovery and margin targets. The service price calculator generates profitable flat-rate fees for diagnostic calls and routine maintenance.
