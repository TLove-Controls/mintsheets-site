⚡ Cooling Efficiency — Sales Tips
  • A 10 SEER → 16 SEER2 upgrade cuts cooling energy use by ~37%. Lead with the dollar amount, not the SEER number.
  • SEER2 ratings are 5–7% lower than old SEER — a 16 SEER2 ≈ 17 SEER under old testing. Never mix old and new ratings in comparisons.
  • Dual-stage and variable-speed units run longer at lower capacity — comfort AND efficiency win.
  • IRA tax credits: up to $600 for qualifying A/C, $2,000 for heat pumps (through 2032).

⚡ Heating Efficiency — Key Facts
  • 80% AFUE → 96% AFUE: saves ~17% of all heating energy. In cold climates that's real money.
  • Heat pumps in mild climates (like AR) can deliver 2–3× more heat per dollar than gas at current rates.
  • IRA rebates: up to $1,750 for heat pump water heater, $8,000 for qualifying heat pump — income-based.
  • Two-stage / modulating furnaces run quieter and more efficiently at low fire 80–90% of the time.
