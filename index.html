What You Can Calculate with MintSheets

MintSheets was built to solve the daily technical and operational challenges faced by HVAC professionals. Instead of relying on guesswork or generic spreadsheets, our suite of specialized HVAC calculators delivers precise, trade-specific answers in seconds.

For field technicians and installers, we offer robust airflow tools. You can quickly perform a CFM calculation to verify room airflow requirements, or use the duct sizing calculator to dimension rigid or flex ductwork based on friction rates. If you need to verify system performance during commissioning or service, the flow rate tool will instantly calculate total system airflow based on sensible temperature rise or static pressure.

For estimators and business owners, protecting margins is critical. We provide comprehensive business tools including an HVAC bid calculator for accurate job costing, a labor burden tool to find your true hourly tech cost, and a service pricing calculator for building profitable flat-rate diagnostic fees.

Why HVAC Pros Use These Calculators

Accuracy and speed are everything in this industry. A minor miscalculation in a manual load assessment can result in an improperly sized system, leading to comfort complaints, high humidity, or premature equipment failure. Similarly, a pricing misstep on a full installation bid can silently erode your net profit margin before the crew even arrives.

Our technical tools, such as the HVAC load calculator and energy savings estimator, are built with logic that aligns with established industry standards like ACCA Manuals J and D. They are designed with a mobile-first UI approach, meaning they load instantly on your phone while you are actively working in a customer's attic or deep inside a commercial mechanical room.

By standardizing how your team performs calculations—whether technical performance or financial modeling—you eliminate rogue variables and ensure consistency across your entire HVAC operation.
