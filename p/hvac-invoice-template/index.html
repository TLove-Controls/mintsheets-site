Product FAQ

Will this auto-calculate totals?
No. Because this is a static Microsoft Word template (DOCX format instead of an Excel format), you must input your subtotal and total numbers manually before presenting it to the customer.
Does this integrate with Quickbooks?
No. This is a standalone document designed to look great for the customer. It does not replace core accounting software syncing. Many contractors generate these clean PDFs to give clients while entering the final numbers structurally into their back-office accounting.
