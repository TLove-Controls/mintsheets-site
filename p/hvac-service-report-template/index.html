Product FAQ

Should I print this or use an iPad?
Both work cleanly. Some contractors print stacks of these to leave on a clipboard in the van, utilizing pen-and-paper in the field. Others open it in Google Docs on their iPad, type the readings in live, and email a PDF directly to the homeowner before leaving the driveway.
Can I tweak the checklist items?
Absolutely. If you want to customize the specific filter sizes or motor checks that your company performs, the native text within the Word document tables can be edited freely to suit your custom standard operating procedures.
